Skill & Project

KMITL +

This Chrome extension, KMITL +, was created with the purpose of redesigning the study schedule and exam table for students at KMITL. Built using Vite, Svelte, and Tailwind, KMITL + offers a sleek and modern interface that is both user-friendly and intuitive. With this extension, students can easily organize their study schedule and keep track of important exam dates. The use of Tailwind ensures that the design is both aesthetically pleasing and responsive, providing a seamless experience for users.
